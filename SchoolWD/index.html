About Us

SchoTest: An initiative of S.J.J. Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is here to fulfill its social responsibility towards the society. We dream to revolutionize the educational system of this era by unleashing the creative potential of the students and help them advance in the educational realm.

At Schotest Public School, we believe in a student-centered approach to learning. Our facilities and resources are designed to support student learning and growth. We have state-of-the-art classrooms, computer labs, science labs, art studios, music rooms, and athletic facilities. Our campus is also equipped with the latest technology to support student learning and research.

At Schotest Public School, we are proud of our students' achievements both inside and outside the classroom. Our students have won numerous academic awards, participated in prestigious competitions, and volunteered their time to help those in need. We are also proud of our strong partnerships with local businesses, organizations, and community leaders, who work with us to support student learning and growth.

Principal Message

"It is easier to build strong children than to repair broken men". A wise quote referring to the role that children could play in laying the foundation of a strong nation. I believe that the foundation of a strong nation depends on the way its children are cared for and nurtured. In order to build a strong nation, we must instill good values in our children providing them love and care, guiding them through thick and thin till they become confident and strong enough. This all can be done through “Quality education”.
